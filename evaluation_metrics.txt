### Evaluation Metrics ###
1. BLEU Score (Bilingual Evaluation Understudy)
- Formula: BLEU = BP × exp(Σ(w_n × log(p_n)))
- Brevity Penalty (BP): Computed dynamically based on hypotheses and references.
- Average BLEU Score: 0.3001

2. Edit Distance (Levenshtein Distance)
- Formula: Minimum single-character edits (insertions, deletions, substitutions).
- Average Edit Distance: 0.1022
